To begin with, you need to determine what exactly you want: to reduce the percentage of body fat and lose weight, or to reduce the percentage of body fat and build muscle. There is a golden rule that not everyone follows. It lies in the fact that the body needs to be given a rest, daily workouts will not accelerate the process of weight loss, much less muscle growth. Balance is important both in nutrition and in training. Discipline is definitely a good thing, but if you feel depressed and don't want to train, give yourself a chance to rest.
